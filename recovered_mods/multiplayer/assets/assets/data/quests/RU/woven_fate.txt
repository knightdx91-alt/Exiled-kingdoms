Kliatva Lyssary
Lyssara khochet prisoedinit'sia k moei gruppe, no snachala mne nuzhno stabilizirovat' tri arkannyh fokusa, razbrosaannyh po zemle.
Ia stabiliziroval khotia by odin arkannyj fokus Lyssary.
Ia zavershil ritual v skrytoi komnate Tremadana.
Vse tri fokusa stabil'ny. Nuzhno vernut'sia k Lyssare v Kingsbridge.
Lyssara zavershila Arkannyj Obet i prisoedinilasj k moei gruppe kak sputnitsa.
